Price Plate

This is my first full stack development project, created in collaboration with four other team members. The app is designed for financially aware grocery shoppers, allowing users to track and understand their spending habits more deeply. The main functionality includes tracking expenses, logging recipe and ingredient costs, and maintaining an inventory of ingredients. Unlike other budget apps, Price Plate lets users calculate specific expenses per serving for recipes and ingredients, offering precise cost insights.

Technologies used: Next.js, Express.js, Node.js, MongoDB, Vercel, TypeScript

QuickBite Prototype

This Figma prototype was created in collaboration with three other members during a 24-hour design-a-thon for UX Fest at Cal Poly. The app is designed to help college students with limited cooking experience and time constraints by offering quick, user-friendly meal recipes. Users can add recipes to a meal planner, create grocery lists, and get personalized recipe recommendations from an AI chatbot based on available ingredients and time. The prototype features a clean and intuitive design, refined through user research and feedback from industry mentors.

Technologies used: Figma, User Research
